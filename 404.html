Instagram Influencer Statistics

By: Peter Mutai
    For the last 20 years, social media platform has been one of the most efficient ways people connect with other people over the internet. Thanks to Influencers, social media keeps growing as we move into the future. Influencers are people who have to build a reputation for their knowledge and expertise on a special topic. For some time now, I have been so curious about someone can grow from zero to million followers. After some research, I found some open-source information about Instagram influencers. For this research, I had to see if the number of followers, or Average likes or Total likes and the number of posts you post can affect your influencer score.

Four common types of Influencers are:

  • Mega or celebrity influencers (1M+ followers)
  • Macro influencers (100K–1M followers)
  • Influencers (10K–100K followers)
  • Nano influencers (1K–10K followers)
