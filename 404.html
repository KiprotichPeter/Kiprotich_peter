    For the last 10 to 20 years, social media has been one of the most efficient ways people connect with other people over the internet. Thanks to Influencer, social media keeps growing as we move into the future. Influencers are people who have build a reputation for their knowledge and expertise on a special topic.

The four common types of Influencers are:

  • Mega or celebrity influencers (1M+ followers)
  • Macro influencers (100K–1M followers)
  • influencers (10K–100K followers)
  • Nano influencers (1K–10K followers)
